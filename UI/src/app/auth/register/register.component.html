Information

Once you create an account, approval will be sent to the admin. If that is approved, your account will be created.

You can maximum 3 books ordered that have not been returned.

You can keep a book with you for maximum 10 days.

If the book is not returned after 10 days, you will receive an email.

After 10th day, a fine of Rs.50 will be imposed everyday and on every book that has not been returned, this fine will be sent to you in the email everyday.

If your fine exceeds Rs.500 or the book is damaged, the Admin can block your account.
